ABOUT US

Christ Our Guiding Light

At Grace Dispensers, we believe that every individual has a unique purpose, and we are here to support you on your spiritual journey. Through our worship services, Bible studies, and community programs, we strive to create an environment where you can encounter God’s presence and experience His transformative power. Our guiding scripture is from Jeremiah 29:11:

“For I know the plans I have for you,” declares the Lord, “plans to prosper you and not to harm you, plans to give you hope and a future.” - Jeremiah 29 : 11
